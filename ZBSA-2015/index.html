What do we offer?

  • reproducible one-click pipelines

  • easy access to data, tools and protocols

  • easy deployment of tools and protocols

  • data integration and management
    +SRA, Genbank upload, UniProt ...

  • data visualisation

  • training

  • compute ressources

What is
